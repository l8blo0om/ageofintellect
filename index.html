— The Stack — 004

The instruments matter less than the hand.

We don't hire tools. We know which blade to use.

IMAGE 18 tools · 40 models

We shift image generation based on the shot: portraiture pulls different models than landscape work.

FLUX.1 Kontext Max → Photorealism, editorial control
Soul Cinema 2.0 → Stylized character work, consistent identity
Topaz Upscale → Final resolution, texture preservation
MOTION 14 tools · 38 models

Shot type determines everything. A 15-second viral clip uses different tools than a 3-minute film.

Kling 3.0 Motion Control → Precise keyframe-based continuity
Google Veo 3.1 → Cinematic quality, long-form narrative
Runway Gen-4.5 → Editorial iteration, rapid revision cycles
SOUND 10 tools · 8 generation systems

We build records, not loops. Full production requires different instruments than a beat starter.

Suno → Full songs with vocals, end-to-end composition
Eleven v3 → Voice synthesis, natural phrasing, multilingual
GarageBand + Magenta → Mastering, production refinement, mixing

Claude → Creative direction, brief analysis, taste documentation
